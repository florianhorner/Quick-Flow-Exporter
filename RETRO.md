## Going forward

Two patterns dominate: subjects too long (4) and the wrong `type:` prefix (3 — `security:`, `quality:`, no prefix). Both are caught by the new commit-msg hook locally; the `type:` issue is the cheaper fix — pick from the allowed set (`feat fix docs style refactor test chore ci build perf revert`) and put the domain in the scope (e.g. `fix(security)` instead of `security:`). The length issue is subject discipline: keep the headline scannable, push the bullet list of what changed into the body.
